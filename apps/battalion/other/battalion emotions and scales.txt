What is MIN?

MIN represents Minutes (time_minutes), which is the duration required to perform that specific action in the game loop.
Example: "Wake Up" has a MIN of 1, while a full work shift or full sleep cycle has a MIN of 480 (8 hours).
What is the number scale of the actions?

The Time scale (time_minutes) runs from 1 to 480 minutes.
The Money scale (money_delta) runs from -500 to +15 (negative numbers represent costs/payments like paying rent, and positive numbers represent income or tips).
All other character delta metrics (discipline, energy, stress, fun, hygiene, social, and health) are small integer values ranging generally from -20 to +10 per action:
Energy delta: -20 to +10
Stress delta: -10 to +8
Social delta: -1 to +10
Health delta: -1 to +7
Hygiene delta: -2 to +10
Fun delta: -3 to +9
Discipline delta: -3 to +8
