How to use these
These override tables should sit above the normal formula as special-case rules. A good pattern is:

Check for an override match first.

If one exists, apply its tighter or looser caps.

Then let the standard tag-and-tier formula finish the calculation.

That gives you a layered system where most actions use the general formula, but emotionally loaded actions can have handcrafted behavior when needed.

Your model is now in a good place for Antigravity-style formulation because it has:
